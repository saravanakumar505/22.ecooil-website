Closed-Loop Oil Recycling

100% EPA-certified waste oil evacuation and re-refining.

Zero Waste. Zero Hassle.

Our proprietary vacuum technology ensures not a single drop hits the ground during extraction.

Vacuum Evacuation

High-powered pumps extract oil directly from the pan without removing the drain plug, preventing spills.

Filtration & Testing

Extracted fluids are immediately tested for contaminants and filtered onboard our specialized trucks.

Re-refining

Waste oil is transported to certified plants where it is re-refined into base oil, closing the loop entirely.
